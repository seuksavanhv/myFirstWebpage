My Equipement.

Film Photography

The Konica Minolta X-700 is my first camera I learned film photography. Paired with Kodak Portra 400 35mm film, it is an amazing combination for great photography for both beginners or professionals. This camera is very common and fairly easy to find on the market.

Instant Film Photography

For instant film photography, i'm using the Fujifilm SQ6. This camera is fun and easy to use. Just load a pack and press the shutter. You can share tangible memories to your friends and families.
